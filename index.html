What You'll Learn

My coaching program is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of chess, from basic principles to advanced strategies.

Opening Principles

Learn how to start your games with confidence. Master opening principles and develop a repertoire tailored to your style.

Tactical Mastery

Discover powerful tactical motifs and improve your calculation skills to find winning combinations in your games.

Strategic Planning

Develop long-term strategic thinking. Understand pawn structures, piece placement, and positional advantages.

Endgame Techniques

Master essential endgame principles and techniques to convert your advantages into wins, even in the most complex positions.

Psychological Preparation

Develop mental toughness and focus. Learn to manage time pressure and maintain composure in critical positions.

Modern Chess Preparation

Learn how to use chess engines and databases effectively. Develop a structured approach to independent study.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my chess coaching services.

Sessions are conducted online via Zoom and a digital chess board. This allows us to analyze positions, play training games, and review concepts in real-time. There is an option for receiving a recording of each session for later review.

For optimal improvement, I recommend one session per week. This gives you enough time to practice the concepts we cover and complete any homework assignments. However, we can adjust the frequency based on your schedule and goals.

It's helpful to have some of your recent games available for us to review. This gives me a better understanding of your playing style and areas for improvement. You should also come prepared with specific goals you'd like to achieve through our coaching sessions.

Life happens! You can reschedule a session with at least 24 hours notice at no additional cost. If you need to reschedule with less notice, I'll do my best to accommodate, but cannot guarantee availability.
